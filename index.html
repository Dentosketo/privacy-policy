Introduction

Qityol Lite ("we", "our", "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

1. Camera Access

We request access to your device's camera solely to allow you to scan ISBN barcodes when adding or identifying books. We do not store or process any images captured by your camera.

2. Microphone Access

We request microphone permission so you can use voice search to search for books, audiobooks, or podcasts. Voice data is processed in real-time and not stored on our servers.

3. Location Access

We use your approximate or precise location to show nearby bookclubs and improve community-based recommendations. Location is never shared with third parties.

4. SMS and Messaging Access

By using our platform(s)"Qityol Mobility" "Qityol Lite" "Heart and Home AFH LLC" "TheVillage", you agree to these terms. We collect and use your mobile phone number solely for account verification and security. We send one-time verification codes (OTP) via text message to confirm your identity during sign-up or login. Your phone number is not used for marketing purposes.

How We Use Your Information

  • To enable ISBN scanning using the camera
  • To power voice search functionality
  • To suggest bookclubs near your current location
  • To verify your identity via SMS for secure login
  • To improve app performance and user experience

Data Storage & Security

We do not store camera or microphone data. Location data is used temporarily and never saved permanently. All other app data, including verification numbers, is securely stored using industry-standard encryption.

Third-Party Services

Qityol Lite may use third-party services such as Firebase, Supabase, or Twilio. These services may collect necessary data to help us provide authentication and improve the app.

Your Rights

You may revoke camera, microphone, location, or messaging permissions at any time from your device settings. Some features may stop working without these permissions.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Updates will appear on this page with the latest “Last Updated” date.
